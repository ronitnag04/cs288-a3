  * The Computer Science Minor 

The minor program is offered through the College of Engineering and is open to all Berkeley undergraduates with a declared major other than EECS or CS. To be admitted to the CS minor, you must have acquired a cumulative grade point average of 2.0 in the required coursework. 

  * The Electrical Engineering & Computer Science Minor 

The EECS minor, offered through the College of Engineering, is an optional program for students declared in another major but interested in coherent EECS study. It is available to all eligible students, regardless of their college. Students interested in only studying computer science should consider the Computer Science Minor. 

  * The Electronic Intelligent Systems Minor 

The minor Electronic Intelligent Systems (EIS) offers the opportunity to gain breadth as well as depth in the area of electronic intelligent systems that connect to the physical and social world
