##  Berkeley EECS Reunion Conference 

The Berkeley EECS Reunion Conference on September 20, 2019, is a community-building and showcase event for undergraduate students and alumni.

##  Berkeley EECS Annual Research Symposium (BEARS) 2020 

The day-long BEARS symposium on Thursday, February 13, 2020, will highlight the latest research in the EECS department and will feature a slate of informative talks by the distinguished faculty members who serve as directors of some of our world-renowned centers and labs.
