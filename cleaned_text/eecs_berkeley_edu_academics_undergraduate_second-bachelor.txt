**How can I study Electrical Engineering and/or Computer Science at Berkeley if I already have an undergraduate degree?**

Applicants who have already earned a bachelor’s degree from an accredited university cannot apply for undergraduate admission to either the College of Engineering or the College of Computing, Data Science, & Society.

One option is to enter graduate-level studies. Once you complete the necessary technical background in mathematics, electrical engineering and/or computer science, it is possible to apply directly to one of our graduate programs. See Recommended Coursework for Applying to the Graduate Degree Programs.

If you have questions about your candidacy for one of our graduate programs, please contact gradadmissions@eecs.berkeley.edu.
